Steak Recipe

The Best Steak You Will Ever Cook!

This recipe will allow you to make a delicious steak in the comfort of your own home

You don't need to visit a fancy resturant to have a good steak.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs New Your Strip Steaks
  • 1/2 Tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp of sea salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 Cloves garlic, peeled and quartered
  • 1 spring fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Pat Steak dry and generously season with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the cast iron with oil. Once hot, add steak
  3. Sear steak for 4 minutes on the first side, flip, and repeat
  4. Using tongs, flip steak onto its side to render off any fat
  5. Reduce heat to medium, add butter, garlic, and rosemary.
  6. Spoon the melted butter sauce over the steak.
  7. Remove steak from pan, let rest for 10 minutes before serving
